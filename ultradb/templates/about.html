About Page

This Web Application will allow a logged-in user to interact with a database.

The database is intended to store the following:

  • Job Sites
  • Areas within a Site
  • Rooms within an Area
  • Job color sheets
  • Paint-Color combinations
  • pytesseract module for Optical Character Recognition to import from paint can lids

Reports generated:

  • Most recent areas touched
  • Areas touched within a specified date range
  • Upcoming areas due for scheduled maintenance
  • What color is room/area x?

In the future, I would also like this to replace the project tracking excel sheet we currently use.

Forms for employees to enter their time into, applying it to specific projects.
